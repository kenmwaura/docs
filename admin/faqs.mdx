The FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions) settings section is a crucial part of your SaaS application's admin panel, enabling administrators to manage and display FAQs on the frontend efficiently. This section is designed to help users find answers to common questions quickly, enhancing their overall experience and reducing the need for direct support
