### How To Setup

#### Step 1: Sign Up for Google Analytics:
- Go to <a href="https://analytics.google.com/" target="_blank">Google Analytics</a> and sign in with your Google account.
- Follow the prompts to create a new account. Enter your website or app name, URL, and other relevant information.

#### Step 2: Get Tracking ID:
- After creating an account, Google Analytics will provide you with a Tracking ID. It looks like UA-XXXXX-Y.
- Copy this Tracking ID as you will need it for integration.
